How Does Each Restaurant Stack Up Against The Rest?


Here's the Very Biased reviews of my top 3 restaurants in Charleston, SC

Bistronomy

Bistronomy is a newer restaurant run by the same great Chef behind the well known "Nico's Oyster bar" in Shem Creek

Slightly North of Broad

A mainstay in the "French Quarter" district of downtown. Slightly North of Broad is well versed in classic French Dining.

Malagon

Why not Spain? Malagon brings the Spanish Tapa scene to the area, crossing styles with local ingredients.
